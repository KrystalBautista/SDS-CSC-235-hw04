US Homicide Patterns Explorer

This visualization explores US homicide data using two linked views: a geographic map of state-level crime distribution and a bar chart showing weapon usage patterns.

How to interact:

  • Click a state on the map to view weapon distribution for that state.
  • Use the year dropdown to filter crime data by year.
  • Hover over states to see total crime counts.
  • Click “Reset” to clear selections and view full data.
